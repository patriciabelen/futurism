II.

Map of Futurism in thirty-eight countries

As a result of the manifestos, Futurist activities spread far beyond its home country of Italy and its origin in France and appeared across Europe and in other parts of the world. Manifestos were translated and published in the media. Letters and press statements were sent to various writers, journalists and artists. Speeches, exhibitions and Futurist happenings took place. Not all responses were positive but the Futurists understood the importance of publicity.
